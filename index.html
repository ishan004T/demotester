About AUOR

AUOR is a multi-disciplinary design studio focused on creating powerful and resonant work. Our practice spans architecture, interiors, branding, and object design. We believe in the power of simplicity, materiality, and form to create lasting and meaningful experiences.

Our approach is collaborative and research-driven, resulting in projects that are conceptually clear and meticulously executed.

Our Philosophy

Our work is guided by a core set of principles that inform every project.

Form

A commitment to pure geometry and structural honesty.

Materiality

An exploration of raw, natural materials and their intrinsic qualities.

Light

The manipulation of light and shadow to define space and evoke emotion.
